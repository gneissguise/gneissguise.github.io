About Me

Hello! My name is Justin, and I'm a software engineer driven by a deep passion for computers, technology, and the continuous pursuit of knowledge. I particularly enjoy exploring new programming languages and paradigms, with a special affinity for building robust and elegant solutions using the Clojure programming language.

Beyond coding, I find great fulfillment in teaching and mentoring, believing strongly in the power of shared knowledge to elevate individuals and the community.

This enthusiasm for creation and collaboration led me to found Monadica, an organization through which I offer services in web-hosting, engage in custom software development, and contribute to open-source projects that benefit the wider tech ecosystem.

I currently reside in Tempe, Arizona.

I have a wonderful wife named Katie, a talkative cat named Atticus, and a playful dog named Tyrion.
